# Welcome to Maryland

Maryland, also known as the "Old Line State," is a state located in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. It is known for its rich history, diverse geography, and vibrant culture.

## Quick Facts

- **Capital**: Annapolis
- **Largest City**: Baltimore
- **State Bird**: Baltimore Oriole
- **State Flower**: Black-Eyed Susan
- **Nickname**: The Free State

## Famous Landmarks

1. **Fort McHenry**  
   Located in Baltimore, this historic fort inspired Francis Scott Key to write "The Star-Spangled Banner."

2. **Chesapeake Bay**  
   The largest estuary in the United States, offering stunning views and fresh seafood.

3. **Antietam National Battlefield**  
   A historic Civil War site commemorating the Battle of Antietam.

## Fun Facts

- Maryland is known for its blue crabs and Old Bay seasoning.
- It was the seventh state to ratify the U.S. Constitution.
- The state has a diverse landscape, ranging from mountains in the west to beaches on the eastern shore.

## Conclusion

Maryland is a small state with a big personality. Whether you're a history buff, a nature lover, or a foodie, Maryland has something for everyone!
