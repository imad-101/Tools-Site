### The Anglo-Saxon Linguistic Legacy

| Aspect      | Old English (450-1150 AD)   | Modern English   |
| ----------- | --------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Alphabet    | þ (thorn), ð (eth), æ (ash) | 26 Latin letters |
| Word Order  | Free                        | SVO dominant     |
| Vocabulary  | 85% Germanic                | 29% Germanic     |

  - Incorrect: "Hē lufodon"
